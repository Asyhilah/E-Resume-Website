About Me

Personal Background

I am Nur Asyhilah Binti Ahmad, currently pursuing my final year of Bachelor of Information Science (Hons) in Library Management at Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM) Kedah Branch. I am originally from Gerik, Perak and live in Kampung Padang Kunyit. Previously, I completed my Sijil Tinggi Persekolahan Malaysia (STPM) at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Seri Budiman in Gerik, Perak, and SPM at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Gerik. Throughout my studies, I have gained basic knowledge in the field of information management, academic research, and essential digital tools such as Microsoft Office and Google Workspace.

Career Goals

My career objective is to become a competent and responsible information professional who contributes positively to the organization and society. I aim to continuously improve my technical skills, communication abilities, and professional ethics in order to meet the demands of the modern working environment.
